How to Play

Flip your opponent's cards. Leave them with nothing.

The Board & Card Types

FLIPA is played on a 4 × 3 grid (12 cells). Cards are placed randomly at the start. Each player has exactly 6 cards: 4 Normal and 2 Figure. Both players choose a skin (character 1–16) that defines how all their cards look.

Normal Card N

When played, flips itself and all adjacent cards. Face-up cards become face-down and face-down cards become face-up.

F
Figure Card F

Select it, then pick any card on the board to flip it. The Figure itself is also flipped face-down after use.

Face-down Card ✕

A card that was flipped during play. Click it on your turn to remove it from the board permanently.

Normal Card

Click any of your Normal cards (pulsing gold outline). It flips itself and every adjacent card. Face-up cards become face-down ✕ and face-down cards flip back up.

Opponent You

Click a pulsing Normal card to play it

Figure Card

Click a Figure card (dashed border, F badge) to select it. Then click any card to flip it. Face-up becomes face-down, face-down becomes face-up. The Figure itself is also flipped face-down after use.

Opponent You

Click a Figure card (dashed border, F badge)

Face-down Card

When it is your turn you can click a face-down card ✕ to remove it from the board permanently. It becomes an empty cell and cannot be affected by Normal cards anymore.

Opponent You

Click any face-down card to remove it from the board

Winning the Game

The game ends when a player has no face-up cards left. That player loses. If both players lose their last face-up card at the same time, it is a Draw.

△ Removing a face-down ✕ card passes your turn. Use that to your advantage.
△ Figure cards are worth more than they seem. They can snipe any card on the board and also swap the turn.

Game Modes

⚙

Training

Play against the bot. Choose Minimal, Medium, or Maximum difficulty. No MMR at stake, perfect for learning.

⚔

Ranked

Matched against a real opponent of similar MMR. Wins gain MMR, losses lose it. You have 60 seconds per turn. Climb the leaderboard.
